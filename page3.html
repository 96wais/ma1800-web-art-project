Facts About Neptune

  • Neptune is the farthest known planet from the Sun.
  • It appears deep blue due to methane in its atmosphere.
  • Winds on Neptune are the fastest in the solar system — over 1,200 mph.
  • It has a dark storm similar to Jupiter’s Great Red Spot.
  • Neptune has 14 known moons, the largest being Triton.
  • Triton orbits backward — the only large moon in the solar system that does.
