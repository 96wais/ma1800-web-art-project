About Saturn

  • Saturn is the sixth planet from the Sun and the second-largest planet in our Solar System.
  • It is a gas giant made mostly of hydrogen and helium.
  • Saturn is famous for its bright, complex ring system made of ice, rock, and dust.
  • A day on Saturn is only about 10.7 hours long.
  • A year on Saturn is about 29 Earth years long.
